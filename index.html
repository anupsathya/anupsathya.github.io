Anup Sathya
Home

Anup is a PhD student at the University of Chicago, advised by Dr. Ken Nakagaki. His research explores how to protect the home from the encroachment of addictive platforms in the attention economy. Believing that learning to calmly coexist with smartphones is one of the defining challenges of the next century, he designs, builds, and studies physical devices that shield us from the constant digital onslaught.

He is the recipient of the 2025 Linda Tischler Award recognizing emerging design talent. His work has received multiple honors, including Fast Company's Innovation by Design Award and Core77's Lifestyle Accessories Award, and has been featured by 404 Media, CBC Radio, IEEE Spectrum, Hackster.io, the Indian Express, and more.
